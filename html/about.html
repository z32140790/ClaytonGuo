ABOUT ME

THIS IS ME AND MY FAMILY

I am Clayton Guo, currently a Junior at Brigham Young University studying Accounting. I was born and raised in Taiwan my whole life. I used to attend local school, where everything was taught in Mandarin and there was only an English class once a week. My life was changed when I got an opportunity to serve a voluntary missionary service in Seattle, Washington for two years since 2017. The experience allowed me to meet people from different countries and I was doing everything in English rather Mandarin. It was a huge transition for me, but little did I know there was a bigger one to come. During my missionary service, despite how much I wanted to study in Taiwan for college, I mustered up the courage and decided to study abroad in the U.S. And that is how I ended up here at BYU right now. It was by far the biggest transition in my life, but it was honestly the best decision I have ever made. I have been such a blessing to be here! The people here are truly one of a kind. I’ve felt so much love from this university and became closer to God. Although sometimes it is hard being in a foreign country alone far from family and home, I’m beyond thankful for this opportunity, this university, the people I’ve met here, my professors, and God. He certainly has prepared the best for me.
